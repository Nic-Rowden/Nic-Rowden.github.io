About Nic

Graphic Design

Starting Graphic Design classes in High School in the year of 2011 has given Nic the chance to graduate from COM with AAS and 5 years of design experince in 2016.

Consistent freelance work from 2016-2020 saw Nic entering the corporate field, working at Simulocity LLC as the head of marketing, content manager of two clients, and a founding team member of PetZone360.

The end of 2022, Nic recieved the oportunity to pay back the education they recieved over the years, and became an instructor a F.I.R.S.T Institute in the GDWD department. Now they use their 12 years of experince to teach the next generation of designers.
