Graphic designis a profession,[2] academic discipline,[3][4][5] and applied art whose activity consists in projecting visual communicationsintended to transmit specific messages to social groups, with specific objectives.[6] Graphic design is an interdisciplinary branch of design[1] and of the fine arts.
